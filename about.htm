Our Vision & Mission

At LKYDG Worries, We are a group of passionate students driven by curiosity and creativity in the field of robotics. With limited resources but unlimited imagination, we’ve taken bold steps to design, build, and showcase innovative robotic projects. From line-following bots to obstacle-avoiders and Bluetooth-controlled machines, every robot we create is a product of late-night brainstorming, endless trials, and our deep love for technology.
